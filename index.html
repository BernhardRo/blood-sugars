About:

A place for me to learn more about diabetes self-management and prediction using CGM data obtained from our Nightscout server. This repo will be used for exploratory purposes. There will eventually be other two other repos. The first will be dedicated to a shiny dashboard. The second will be for an R package I intend to develop by the end of 2015. When they are ready, I'll publish links to them here and demo their usage.
